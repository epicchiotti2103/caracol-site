AppsFlyer or Adjust: which Mobile Measurement Partner (MMP) fits your tech stack?

Compare features, integrations, and costs to make the best decision for your mobile marketing stack.

Why this decision is critical

The global mobile-attribution software market is growing 13 % per year and is expected to reach US $ 10.9 billion by 2033. With the new privacy policies in iOS 17 and Android Privacy Sandbox, user tracking has been limited, reducing attribution accuracy by up to 25 % for apps that don’t use robust tools.

  • 28 % accuracy loss post-iOS 14.5
  • Up to US $ 50 k per year in re-integration costs
  • Weeks of development wasted on incompatible SDKs

AppsFlyer

  • Unlimited exports and full data control
  • Regulated industries (fintech, health)

Frequently asked questions

Which integrates better with Facebook Ads and Google Ads?
Both have official partnerships; AppsFlyer offers more integrations, Adjust goes deeper on the main ones.
How to migrate without losing history?
Export 90 days of data, run both SDKs in parallel for 30 days, then switch after calibration.

Conclusion

Delivery apps and startups: choose AppsFlyer. Fintechs, gaming and large advertisers: Adjust delivers more long-term value.
